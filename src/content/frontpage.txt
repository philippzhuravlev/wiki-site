# Welcome to the Wiki!

What if the gods were a bunch of etymology nerds and linguistic purists?

Urheimat (from German Urheimat, “original homeland”) is the working title name given to a fantasy/sci-fi world, heavily inspired by Dungeons and Dragons and Europa Universalis IV

This wiki is a work in progress and is not yet complete.

## How to use this wiki

This wiki is built with Next.js and React. It uses the [Markdown](https://www.markdownguide.org/) format for content.
